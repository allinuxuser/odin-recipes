Half-Boiled Eggs

Ya Kun Kaya Toast, Singapore City, Singapore by Andrew Annuar used under CC-BY-NC-SA 2.0, full terms of license here

Ahh, half-boiled eggs. a staple of the singaporean breakfast and a loved dish by many. And you can make it at home! I'll teach you.

Ingredients

  • White pepper
  • Salty soy sauce
  • One or two eggs (depending on how hungry you are)

Start by boiling some water, whether it be by kettle or boiling a pot of water on the stove. Once the water has finished boiling, kill the heat if you're using a stove and drop your egg(s) in the kettle or pot and let it cook for 6 minutes.

Once the 6 minutes are up, fish your eggs out and crack them into a bowl or plate, season with salty soy sauce and white pepper to taste and voila! A tasty breakfast, enjoy!
